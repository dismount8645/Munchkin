# Future Ambitions

These are high-level goals to be tackled after the core turn loop is stable.

## Level 10 Win Condition
- [ ] Ensure the game ends immediately when a player kills a monster to reach Level 10.
- [ ] Prevent Level 10 from being reached via "Selling Items" or "Go Up a Level" cards (max at 9).

## Cooperative Combat
- [ ] Implement the "Ask for Help" prompt during combat.
- [ ] Allow other players to add their `Combat Strength` to the active player's.
- [ ] Handle "Treasure Sharing" negotiations.

## Advanced Rules
- [ ] **Monster Enhancers**: Logic to play cards that buff/debuff monsters mid-fight.
- [ ] **Class Abilities**: Specific logic for Warrior (wins ties), Thief (steals/backstabs), etc.
- [ ] **Selling Items**: Discard items totaling 1000 Gold to gain 1 Level (up to Level 9).
